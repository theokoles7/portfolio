Gallery

Welcome to what we will call my "art gallery". I don't typically show people my art, because I don't find it so impressive. However, one of my good friends recently pointed out a character trait of mine of trying to always be a perfectionist, and although I'm sure she did not intend it as such, I took it as a challenge. Therefore, I chose to take on that challenge by making my art public, no matter what I think about it. Enjoy!
